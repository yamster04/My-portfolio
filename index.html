About Me

Based in Digos City, Philippines, I'm a video editor with hands-on experience in creating engaging and well-paced videos. I focus on clean edits, good timing, and using music to help tell a clear and emotional story.

"I believe the best edits feel like a memory. Every cut and moment is shaped to tell a story you can feel, not just watch."

With intermediate expertise in Capcut Pro and DaVinci Resolve, I specialize in creating content that connects with audiences on a deeper level. Whether it's for businesses, influencers, or musicians, I focus on delivering edits that capture the essence of your message.

Currently available for freelance projects and excited to collaborate with creators who value quality, emotion, and storytelling in their visual content.
